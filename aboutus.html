About Scentiment

Scentiment is more than just a candle brand — it is a feeling. We believe that scent has the power to transform a space and uplift the soul.

The name Scentiment comes from the words “scent” and “sentiment,” reflecting our mission to connect fragrance with emotion.

Our candles are crafted to bring warmth, relaxation, and comfort into everyday life.
